How JobConnect Works

Our platform streamlines the recruitment process, making it easier for job seekers and employers to connect.

Create Your Profile

Sign up as a job seeker or employer and create your personalized profile to get started.

Upload Your Resume

Job seekers can upload their resumes to showcase their skills and experience to potential employers.

Connect With Opportunities

Employers can browse resumes and connect with potential candidates that match their needs.

Who Uses JobConnect?

Our platform serves both job seekers and employers with tailored experiences.
